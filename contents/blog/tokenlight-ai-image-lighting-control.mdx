TokenLight is a research demo that shows how short text tags - called attribute tokens - can dramatically change the lighting in an AI-generated image. By simply typing a prompt and adding light-specific words, you can tweak the look with softer light, side light, or warm tones. It aims to give you quick and clear control over light direction, strength, and mood without requiring deep photography skills.

## What are Attribute Tokens?

Modern image models read text and turn it into pictures. Attribute tokens are short words that point the model to a specific light effect. They act like switches for direction, tone, and power. 

By using specific tokens for light, this method keeps prompts short and easy to manage. You avoid long, convoluted prompt tricks and still get steady, predictable output. This makes lighting control more direct and repeatable across multiple generations.

## Key Features and Use Cases

TokenLight provides several ways to manipulate the scene:
- **Simple Light Words**: Add specific tokens to your prompt to change the scene light instantly.
- **Directional Control**: Specify lighting from the left, right, front, or back.
- **Strength Adjustments**: Move seamlessly from a soft glow to a stronger, harsher light.
- **Mood Tweaks**: Apply warm or cool tones that completely shift the feel of the image.

**Common Use Cases:**
- **Product Photography**: Make product shots brighter with front light or add side light to emphasize shape and texture.
- **Lifestyle Photos**: Add a warm room feel for a cozy vibe, or a cool tone for tech and modern shots.
- **Portraits**: Nudge portraits to have softer light for smooth skin, or stronger light for dramatic, high-contrast looks.

## How to Get Started with TokenLight

Using TokenLight is straightforward and designed for quick iteration:

1. **Open the Project Page**: Find the interactive demo on the TokenLight research page.
2. **Type your Subject**: Keep your core subject prompt short and clear.
3. **Add Light Tokens**: Append a few light tokens such as `soft light` or `side light`.
4. **Generate and Review**: Generate the image and review the results. 

*Pro tip*: Try changing one token at a time to see its exact effect. Keep your subject words steady while you test different light tokens to isolate what works best. Save your best prompt recipes to reuse across different projects!

## FAQ

**What is an attribute token?**
An attribute token is a specific, short text phrase used in a prompt that reliably triggers a specific stylistic or lighting effect in the AI model.

**Can I use TokenLight for both new images and edits?**
Yes! TokenLight works with both new images generated from text (text-to-image) and with edits applied to images you upload (image-to-image).

**Do I need professional photography skills?**
Not at all. The tokens use plain English (like "soft light" or "warm tone"), making it accessible for anyone to achieve professional-looking lighting.
