# Vision Banana: The New Computer Vision Paradigm by Google DeepMind

## What is Vision Banana

Vision Banana is the new and revolutionary model developed by Google DeepMind. Unlike classic specialized models, it is a single model capable of tackling multiple computer vision tasks (segmentation, detection, depth estimation) through a generalist text-to-image approach.

## How the Model Works

The heart of Vision Banana lies in its ability to interpret vision tasks as generation prompts. Instead of having multiple heads for different tasks, it leverages a unified architecture where visual input and textual prompt generate the desired output directly in image format.

## The Benefits of a Generalist Approach

This approach offers enormous advantages. The reduction of complexity and the ability to perform zero-shot generalization on visual tasks makes it a game changer for those developing AI agents and using models like Google Gemini or custom solutions based on OpenClaw.
