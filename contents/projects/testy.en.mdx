TestyPrep (Testy) is a cutting-edge B2B SaaS platform designed for **training schools**, corporate academies, and brands looking to provide a top-tier learning and assessment experience.

Through interactive training tools, advanced test management, and exam simulations, Testy enables institutions to digitize their evaluation processes, track student progress, and significantly improve success rates.
